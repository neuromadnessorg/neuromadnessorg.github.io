The best natural treatment for Lyme disease

Lyme Disease affects millions every day, it's a poorly understood debilitating illness **caused by a bacterium called Borrelia Burgdorferi sensu lato**. It can mimic or cause all sorts of more commonly known syndromes, such as Multiple Sclerosis[1], ALS[2][3], Rheumatoid Arthritis[1], or Alzheimer[5]. It's a very complex illness, and if it wasn't enough, it usually presents itself with a host of other infectious agents, such as Bartonella sp, Babesia sp, Mycoplasma sp, Chlamydia and others, making the clinical picture in most cases very insidious, unspecific and thus difficult to diagnose.
